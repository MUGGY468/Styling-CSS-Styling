My Biography and Things About Me

By Daniel Salinas

Early Life

Daniel was born on October 8, 2005, and was taken to Ecuador in 2009 because his parents wanted him to grow up with their culture. Daniel stayed there, made a lot of friends he still talks to, and learned English by watching a TON of movies. When Daniel arrived in the US, arguably the biggest country, Daniel knew what he wanted to do but just didn't know how to get there yet.

Higher Education

After working in TV production programs for a few years, Daniel realized on his graduation day that he wanted to make VFX, which led him to attend UCF. There, he was introduced to new possibilities in cinema-making, which he hadn't considered before, like traditional 2D animation. This realization rekindled a childhood love for drawing. Driven by this rediscovered passion, Daniel now spends about 3-4 hours a day drawing intensively, hoping to develop enough skill to be employed in animation for a cinema production company someday.

Future Goals

As Daniel mentioned before, he aspires to become an animator. After graduation, he plans to go directly to California to work at Disneyland to save money for a master's degree or to find a job. If he secures a job in California, he will gladly accept it and try to climb the ranks. If he finds a remote job, he intends to return to Ecuador, where rent is much cheaper and the quality of life is higher, to live comfortably while working as an animator. He also hopes to raise his child the same way he was raised, in the very same place where it all started.
